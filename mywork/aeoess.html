The Vision

√ÜOESS isn't a meditation app in hardware form. It's physical, measurable, repeatable performance training ‚Äî treating breath the way a gym treats muscles. The diaphragm is muscle tissue. It responds to progressive resistance.

The device creates calibrated resistance during inhalation and exhalation. Sensors capture airflow patterns. The AI coach interprets patterns, detects dysfunction, and guides users through personalized training cycles.

Long-term vision: An always-on companion that understands your physiological state ‚Äî stress patterns, emotional regulation, performance readiness ‚Äî and helps you consciously control what was previously unconscious.

  • Industrial design isn't decoration ‚Äî it's core to user adoption
  • Sensor selection and signal processing are their own deep domains
  • The wellness hardware market is crowded but the breath training niche is wide open
  • Sometimes the timing isn't right, and that's okay

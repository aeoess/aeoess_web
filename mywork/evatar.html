The Problem

Social media is essential but exhausting for founders and small teams. It demands:

  • constant content ideation and planning
  • platform-specific formatting and optimization
  • consistent posting across multiple channels
  • keeping a coherent voice while staying authentic
  • ongoing replies to comments and DMs

Most people either neglect social entirely or burn out trying to keep up. Existing tools either only schedule posts or become yet another system that needs managing.

Eva ‚Äî Voice-to-Content Creative Assistant

  • turns voice notes into scripts, posts and video narratives
